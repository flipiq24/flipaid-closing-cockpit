Intro to COMMAND
COMMAND is not a magic wand. It’s a tool that does three things better than any other system — and your job is to run all three, every day.
1
Makes you 3× more efficient.

Everything you need lives in one place — finding deals, comping, agent data, offers, follow-up. No jumping between the MLS, spreadsheets, a dialer, and a CRM. That’s how you push at least 100 offers a month, three times what you’d do stitching separate tools together. Volume is the game, and efficiency is how you win it.

2
Builds the agent relationships that feed you.

Deals don’t come from luck — they come from relationships. COMMAND helps you build and work 100 agent relationships around how they make money, not by begging them for deals. Nurture those 100 all year and they start bringing the deals to you, over and over, without you chasing.

3
Chases deals based on seller propensity.

You don’t chase what everyone else is chasing. COMMAND ranks deals by the seller’s propensity to sell — who’s actually motivated — so your calls land on the deals most likely to move. It’s data no one else has, and you can’t get it off the MLS.

System Navigation
Where everything lives. This is your map for Week 1 so you never hunt for a tool.
Today's Plan
Today's Tasks
The day's work list pulled straight from your plan.
Today’s Tasks breaks your plan into the individual actions to complete today — calls to make, campaigns to send, responses to work, deals to follow up. Each task ties back to a deal or an agent so you can act on it in one click. Finishing your tasks every single day is where the discipline shows up.
My Deals
Your full list of every deal you're working.
My Deals opens your complete pipeline — every deal you’re working, in one place. Filter, sort, and triage from here; it’s the home base for follow-up and Deal Review.
Communications
Inbox
Replies from your text and email campaigns land here.
The Inbox is the unified stream of every reply from your outreach — texts and emails from agents across all your campaigns in one place. Sort by sentiment and always call the negatives back; respond fast and log the next step.
Find Deals
MLS Search
Advanced MLS search where you build and save filters to find deals.
MLS Search is your engine for pulling deals off the market on your terms. Build filters by area, price, days on market, status, and type — then save them so your daily search is one click. High Propensity and Hot Deals live here too.
MLS Search › High Propensity
Pre-selected properties ranked by the seller's propensity to sell — work the highest first.
High Propensity surfaces now-or-aged actives, pendings, and backups where the seller is most likely to move. Open on the seller’s pain point, use Property Intelligence, tax data, and skip tracing to gauge feasibility, and build your agent pipeline. Work the highest propensity-to-sell first.
MLS Search › Hot Deals
The highest-signal new MLS listings worth chasing.
Hot Deals surfaces the strongest new listings on the MLS right now — the highest-signal deals worth your time so you’re not scrolling the whole market every morning.
MLS Search › My Favorites
The listings you've starred to come back to.
My Favorites collects every listing you’ve starred so you can jump straight back to the deals you’re tracking without re-running a search.
Agent Search
Find and filter investor-friendly agents to target.
Agent Search is how you build your 100 relationships. Find and filter agents by production, transaction history, and how they do business, then work them into Hot / Warm / Cold / Unknown. Prioritize the high producers and track every touch.
Agent Search › Send Agent Campaign
Blast text or email out to your agent list.
Send Agent Campaign lets you run bulk outreach to the agents you’ve filtered — pick a template, choose your audience, and send text or email to stay top of mind at scale.
Agent Search › My Priority Agents
Your top agents — the relationships you work first.
My Priority Agents is your short list of the highest-value agents — the ones you call and nurture first because each one can feed you deals all year.
Agent Search › Campaign Tracking
Track responses and performance on your agent campaigns.
Campaign Tracking shows how your agent outreach is landing — who opened, who replied, and where each agent stands — so you can work the warmest responses first.
Tools
DispoPro
Where you sell a property you have under contract.
DispoPro is the disposition side — once a deal is under contract, this is where you sell or assign it. Find buyers and investor-friendly agents, blast the deal out, manage offers, and move to assignment or double-close. Acquisition gets it under contract; DispoPro gets it closed.
DispoPro › Sell a Property
Push a deal under contract out to buyers and investors to sell it.
Sell a Property runs the disposition flow — search your investor and buyer database, filter by strategy and purchase range, and market the deal to the buyers most likely to close it fast.
My Stats
Your performance dashboard — your KPIs vs. targets.
My Stats is your scoreboard. It shows your real numbers against target — logins, outreach, offers, calls, deals worked, and closes. Review it at end of day; the north star is 2 closed deals a month.

The round support bubble (bottom-right) and the orange help button open help and support — ask a question or send feedback without leaving your work.

Daily Reps
Your daily plan — the five sections you run every single day, top to bottom, in priority order. Today you learn them by doing them.
1
Deals in Working.

On Process, Infraction, Automated, Resurfaced. Work the Infractions first (past contact threshold), then re-engage Resurfaced, then check Automated runs.

2
Agents › Text and Email Campaigns.

Send today’s outreach across Hot, Warm, Cold, and Unknown — stay top of mind with the agents where you’ve planted the seed.

3
Agents › Campaign Responses.

Work the agents who replied — Positive first, then Neutral, then Negative. Always call the negatives back; you’ll be surprised what you get.

4
Agents › Priority Calls.

Your 100 highest-value agents — the ones who each feed you four deals a year. Call to stay relevant and present. Nothing matters more.

5
New Deals › New High Propensity to Sell Deals.

30 queued. Call agents on pre-selected actives, pending, or backup deals; open on the seller’s pain point and use Property Intelligence.
